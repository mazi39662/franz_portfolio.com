IN-DEPTH: THE STORY BEHIND THE CODE

Hello, I'm Terence Franz Indenerio, and I am passionate about problem solving and coding. In my free time, I enjoy drawing and playing online games like CODM to unwind. I am a proud graduate of BSIT from Palompon Institute of Technology.

During my time in college, I always sought out fellow coding enthusiasts, as there were only a few at my school who shared the same interest. Despite the limited resources, I managed to create mobile games and continuously expand my programming knowledge.

I often wish there was a larger group of programmers at my school, but I make the most of the opportunities available. Programming brings me immense joy, to the point where I can spend hours fully immersed in coding, entering a flow state.

It empowers me to bring my ideas to life, limited only by my imagination. However, there is a lingering fear in my mind that I might not be good enough for the programming industry. Nevertheless, I am determined to learn everything I need to grow and overcome any obstacles in my path.
